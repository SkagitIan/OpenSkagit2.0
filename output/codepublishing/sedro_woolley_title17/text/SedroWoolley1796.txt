        17.96.010  Adopted shoreline master program.

             The city of Sedro-Woolley shoreline master program is set forth in Exhibit 1 attached to Ordinance No. 1983-21. A copy of the Sedro-Woolley shoreline master program (and any amendments thereto) is available at the office of the Sedro-Woolley planning department. (Ord.  1983-21  § 1, 2021; Ord.  1847-16  § 2 (part), 2016)

        17.96.020  Violations—Penalties.

             Any person violating or failing to comply with any of the provisions contained in this chapter shall be subject to the enforcement provisions contained in Title  18 , Code Enforcement. (Ord.  1847-16  § 2 (part), 2016)

        17.96.030  Fees.

             Fees associated with applications for actions under the shoreline management program shall be assessed as designated in the current city of Sedro-Woolley building, planning and engineering fee schedule, as adopted by resolution of the city council and on file with the city clerk. (Ord.  1847-16  § 2 (part), 2016)
