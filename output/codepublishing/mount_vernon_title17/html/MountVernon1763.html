17.63.010 Special use permit required.

No uses shall be permitted and no buildings or premises shall hereafter be erected in the F-1 district without a special use permit from the city council on recommendation from the hearing examiner. The city council may issue a permit for a proposed use in the F-1 district provided:

A. A hearing has been held by the hearing examiner and the Corps of Engineers, the Federal Emergency Management Administration, and other state and federal agencies with jurisdiction concur in approving the use;

B. The permit to be granted requires that the proposed use complies with all regulations of the Corps of Engineers, the Federal Emergency Management Administration, and other state and federal agencies with jurisdiction concur in approving the use.

C. The permit is subject to the receipt of all other state or federal permits which may be required. Documentation of their receipt shall be filed with the city. The permit may then be reviewed by the city if the other state or federal permits substantially change the proposed development. (Ord. 2997 § 8, 2000).
