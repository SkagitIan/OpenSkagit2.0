        17.117.010  Applicability of provisions.

             Zoning rules and regulations applicable to any property within the city shall, from and after the effective date hereof, be as set forth herein. The ordinance codified in this title shall not affect the zoning classification of any parcel of property within the city, and reclassification shall, from and after the effective date hereof, be made solely as provided in Chapter  17.111  MVMC, herein; provided, however, that properties heretofore designated as R-1, 8.4 shall hereafter be designated R-1, 7.6, and shall be subject to the rules applicable thereto; and provided further, that properties heretofore designated as C-3 shall hereafter be designated C-4, and shall be subject to the rules applicable thereto, and provided further, that properties heretofore designated as C-1 shall hereafter be designated as C-la, and shall be subject to the rules applicable thereto. (Ord. 2352, 1989).
