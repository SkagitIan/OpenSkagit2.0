        17.03.010  Citation.

             This title shall be known and may be cited as “the zoning code of the city of Mount Vernon.” (Ord. 3315, 2006; Ord. 2352, 1989).

        17.03.020  Purposes.

             The provisions of land use adopted and established in this title are intended to promote the most appropriate and compatible uses of the land within the city; prevent undue concentration of population and avoid overcrowding of land; encourage coordinated development of unbuilt areas; conserve or restore natural beauty and other natural resources; preserve areas and buildings of architectural, cultural or historic significance; facilitate the orderly development and use of transportation, water, sewage and other public services; provide adequate light and air; lessen traffic congestion; and secure safety from fire. (Ord. 3315, 2006; Ord. 2352, 1989).
