How the App Treats Records

Recorded documents Only filings attached to an active assessor parcel are counted, shown, or used in the sync brief.
Generated opportunities Generated opportunity queries are read-only parcel screens over allowed tables and must return parcel numbers.
Opportunity tabs Tabs are ranked screens, not legal findings. They combine land use, value, tax, zoning, improvement, utility, and map signals.
