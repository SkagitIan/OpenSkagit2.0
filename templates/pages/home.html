How it works

Built from public data. Organized by place.

OpenSkagit connects public records across parcels, neighborhoods, and districts so local patterns are visible, understandable, and easier to act on.

Collect and connect

Records from permits, land use, assessments, and civic activity are normalized into one coherent local system.

Map the signals

Every record is tied to place, making it easier to spot where growth, risk, and policy pressure are concentrating.

Explain clearly

Raw tables are translated into plain-language insights with transparent logic and clear local context.
