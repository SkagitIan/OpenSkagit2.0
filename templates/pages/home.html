Most of it lives in formats nobody can actually use.

Who it's for

Built for everyone who works with Skagit.

Resident

Understand who owns the land around you, what's being built nearby, and how your property taxes are spent.

"Who owns the parcel at 1234 Cook Rd?"

Local Leader

Track development trends in your district and understand the full fiscal picture at a glance.

"What's happening to development in my district?"

Researcher

Cross-reference data layers and export structured results for deeper analysis.

"Show taxing district overlap vs. housing density"

County Employee

Surface records faster and answer constituent questions without digging through legacy systems.

"What parcels are flagged for reassessment this cycle?"
